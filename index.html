So Sweet,
Southern Confections

These are a southern delicacy, hand crafted in the Mile High City! My New Orleans style pecan pralines are one of a kind. I only use premium ingredients, along with fresh and delicious pecans that are shipped directly from a small family farm in Alabama.

I have adapted the New Orleans style recipe and incorporated techniques that my family have used in their own praline recipe for generations. The resulting product is a candy that is so fresh and delicate that it melts in your mouth while offering a buttery crunch from the pecans. These pralines are made with fresh Alabama pecans and are a unique and decadent treat. I am truly proud of what I have crafted and I hope you enjoy them as well!
